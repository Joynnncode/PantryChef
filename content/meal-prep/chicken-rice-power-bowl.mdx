This is the "power bowl" pattern: a grain, a protein, and a vegetable, built once and portioned into ready-to-go meals for the week. It's built directly on top of the [Chicken Breast](/meal-prep/chicken-breast), [Brown Rice](/meal-prep/brown-rice), and [Roasted Vegetables](/meal-prep/roasted-vegetables) guides — if you're already batch-cooking those three, assembling this bowl adds almost no extra work.

## Tips

- Keep the sauce separate until serving — dressed rice gets soggy by day three.
- For a lower-calorie, cutting-focused version, swap half the rice for extra vegetables and keep the chicken portion the same — same protein, fewer carbs.
- Reheat the chicken and rice together, but add any raw or delicate toppings (herbs, avocado, a fried egg) fresh.
- Rotate the vegetable and sauce each week to keep the same base from feeling repetitive.
