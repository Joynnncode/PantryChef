A big batch of brown rice is the backbone of easy weeknight meals — it's the neutral base that lets your proteins and vegetables rotate without repeating the same meal twice.

## Tips

- Cook a full rice-cooker batch on Sunday and portion into containers immediately.
- Spread rice on a sheet pan to cool quickly before refrigerating — this prevents sogginess.
- Freezes exceptionally well in flat, single-portion bags that thaw in minutes.
- Add a squeeze of lime or a drizzle of sesame oil after reheating to freshen the flavor.
