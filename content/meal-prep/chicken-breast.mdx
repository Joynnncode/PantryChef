Chicken breast is one of the most reliable meal-prep proteins: it's cheap, cooks fast in bulk (sheet pan, grill, or instant pot), and takes on almost any marinade or spice blend.

## Tips

- Slice or shred right after cooking while it's still warm — it's easier to portion.
- Store in an airtight container with a spoonful of cooking liquid or sauce to keep it from drying out in the fridge.
- Freeze in individual portions so you can thaw only what you need.
- Pair with a grain and a roasted vegetable for a complete meal-prep bowl.
