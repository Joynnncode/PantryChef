Skipping the bread keeps this both fast to prep and light on calories relative to its protein content, which is exactly the trade-off that matters most on a cutting week. Keeping the lettuce separate from the filling until you eat is the only thing standing between this and a soggy, wilted wrap.

## Tips

- Canned salmon or canned chicken work as drop-in substitutes if you want variety through the week.
- The paper-towel trick for the lettuce matters more than it seems — wet leaves wilt within a day.
- Add a handful of avocado right before eating for healthy fats, rather than mixing it in ahead of time (it browns).
- Pack the tuna mix and lettuce in the same container with a divider if you don't want to carry two containers.
