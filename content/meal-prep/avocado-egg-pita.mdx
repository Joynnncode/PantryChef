This is the classic "assemble once, eat all week" combo meal: a creamy avocado-egg filling that keeps well on its own, paired with pita and sauce that you add fresh right before eating. Keeping the wet filling separate from the bread until serving time is the one trick that makes this hold up for days instead of going soggy by day two.

## Tips

- Store the filling and pita separately — combine only at serving time.
- Press plastic wrap directly onto the surface of the avocado mix before sealing the container; it slows browning noticeably.
- Swap the eggs for canned chickpeas (mashed) for a vegan version, or add a scoop of cottage cheese for even more protein.
- Works just as well in a wrap, over toast, or spooned onto crackers if you're out of pita.
- Pair with the [Everyday Yogurt-Tahini Sauce](/meal-prep/everyday-yogurt-tahini-sauce) for a tangy finish, or use hot sauce, tzatziki, or hummus instead.
