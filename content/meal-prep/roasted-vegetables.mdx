Roasted vegetables — broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, zucchini, or whatever's in season — are the easiest way to add fiber and color to every meal-prep container.

## Tips

- Cut vegetables to a similar size so they roast evenly.
- Toss with oil, salt, and pepper only — save fresh herbs or sauces for after reheating so they stay vibrant.
- Keep firmer vegetables (carrots, broccoli) separate from softer ones (zucchini, tomatoes) since they hold up differently over the week.
- Great cold in a grain bowl or reheated as a side.
