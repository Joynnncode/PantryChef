Overnight oats are the lowest-effort meal-prep item on this list — just oats, liquid, and a fridge.

## Tips

- Use a 1:1 ratio of rolled oats to milk (dairy or plant-based), plus a spoonful of yogurt or chia seeds for extra creaminess.
- Prep 5 jars at once on Sunday — same base, different toppings each day (berries, banana, nut butter, cinnamon).
- Add mix-ins like nuts or granola right before eating so they stay crunchy instead of going soft overnight.
- Doesn't freeze well due to texture changes — stick to the 5-day fridge window.
