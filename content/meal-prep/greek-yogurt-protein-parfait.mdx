Layered parfaits solve the usual meal-prep breakfast problem — cereal goes soggy, yogurt alone gets boring — by keeping the crunchy element separate until the moment you eat. It's also one of the highest protein-per-calorie combo meals here, which makes it a good anchor for a cutting-focused week.

## Tips

- Nonfat plain Greek yogurt has roughly double the protein of flavored yogurt for the same calories — sweeten it yourself instead.
- Frozen berries work fine and are cheaper; just thaw briefly or eat them semi-frozen for texture.
- If you're not tracking calories closely, swap the honey for a mashed banana layer instead.
- Doesn't freeze — Greek yogurt's texture breaks down and turns grainy after thawing.
